Choose mode, fill extra fields as needed and click Compute EA.
This demo covers the classic 68000 addressing modes: Data/Address register direct; (An), (An)+, -(An), (d16,An), (d8,An,Xn), absolute short/long, PC-relative (d16,PC) and (d8,PC,Xn), and immediate. It computes Effective Address (EA) and shows operand bytes from the memory you set. Not a full CPU emulator, but a learning aid.
